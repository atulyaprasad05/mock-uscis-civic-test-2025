Mock USCIS Civics Test

2025 (128-question) practice exam

How it works

  • You will be asked 20 random questions drawn from a pool of 128.
  • Questions are picked proportionally across all civics topics.
  • Scoring is 1 point for a fully correct answer, 0 otherwise — no partial credit.
  • You must answer at least 12 out of 20 correctly to pass.
  • Some questions are state-specific or about current officials — for those, you self-assess your answer.

Source: USCIS publication M-1778 (09/25) — 128 Civics Questions and Answers (2025 version).
